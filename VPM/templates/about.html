Hello folks! I'm Apoorvi.

Welcome to VPM (Visual Product Matcher). It is simple yet powerful tool that helps you find products similar to an image you upload or provide via URL.

Our goal is to make product discovery easier and faster. Your shopping experience for fashion apparels with VPM gets simplified as it uses advanced image feature extraction and similarity matching techniques to show you the closest matches.

The platform is built with Django and leverages machine learning features to compare products across categories. We focus on providing a clean, minimal, and user-friendly interface for a smooth experience.

This project is currently in development. In the coming version it will have better accuracy, more categories, and enhanced the overall design.

Thank you for trying VPM. I hope it makes your product search effortless!
